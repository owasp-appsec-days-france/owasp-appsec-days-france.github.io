OWASP Days France 2025

OWASP AppSec Days France 2025 is the first local OWASP conference organized in Paris, France. It will take place on Tuesday, September 23, 2025. It will be a conference featuring presentations from renowned European speakers and experts. We hope to welcome over 150 security professionals for this day that promises to be full of opportunities.

Call for Presentations

The OWASP AppSec Days conference is a conference on application security. It welcomes topics aimed at IT professionals, specializing in security as well as in development, testing, exploitation, or management and governance.

CfP to open soon!

Program

For this first session, this event will take place over one day: on Tuesday, September 23, 2025. During this day, you will be able to attend a series of exciting technical and non-technical conferences in the field of application security.

About

OWASP is a global independent community aimed at making the importance of web application security "visible" by disseminating know-how in developing and operating secure web applications and making it freely available. All OWASP tools, such as documents, videos, slides, podcasts, etc., can be used for free under a free license.

OWASP is free, open, and independent of any company. Anyone interested is invited to work on projects or simply participate in knowledge sharing.
